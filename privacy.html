Privacy and analytics

Visitor information.

This site is designed as an academic portfolio. It does not collect personal information through forms or create user accounts.

Analytics

Visitor analytics are currently not active. When enabled, the site will use privacy-focused aggregate analytics to measure page visits, referral sources, browser type, and broad geographic trends. Analytics cannot reliably identify a visitor's name, email address, or private identity.

External links

Links to GitHub, LinkedIn, article DOIs, and other external sites are governed by their own privacy practices.
