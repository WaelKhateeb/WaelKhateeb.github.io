Privacy and analytics

Visitor information

This website is an academic profile. It does not require account registration and collects only the aggregate, privacy-respecting analytics described below.

Analytics

What is measured

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how the site is used. It records aggregate information such as page views, approximate (city/region-level) location, referral source, device and browser type, and a small number of interaction events: opening a presentation PDF, clicking an outbound link, and submitting the contact form.

IP addresses are anonymized, and the analytics are configured for aggregate reporting. Aggregate website analytics cannot reliably identify a visitor's name, email address, or private identity.

Cookies and opting out

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish returning visits. You can block analytics by using your browser's tracking-protection settings, an ad/tracker blocker, or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. The site remains fully functional with analytics blocked.

Contact form

The contact form is delivered to my email through the third-party service FormSubmit. The name, email address, and message you submit are used solely to read and reply to your message and are handled under FormSubmit's privacy practices.

External links

Links to journal DOI pages, Google Maps, Google Scholar, GitHub, LinkedIn, and other external sites are governed by the privacy practices of their respective providers.
