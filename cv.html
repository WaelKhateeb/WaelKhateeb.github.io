Research Profile

Applied mathematician working in nonlinear dynamics, mathematical biology, scientific computing, and machine learning. Research interests include high-codimension bifurcation theory, delayed systems, predator-prey and epidemic models, reaction-diffusion systems, physics-informed neural networks, inverse problems, and topological methods for dynamical data.
