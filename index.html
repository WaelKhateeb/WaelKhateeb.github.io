Research profile

Rigorous mathematics for structured biological and computational systems.

My work combines bifurcation theory, delay equations, reaction-diffusion models, data-driven methods, and machine learning to analyze how complex systems change, organize, and form patterns.

Research areas.

01

Nonlinear dynamics & bifurcation theory

High-codimension bifurcations, Bogdanov-Takens and nilpotent singularities, Hopf bifurcation, heteroclinic loops, and the qualitative structure of delayed and ecological systems.
