Research focus

Where the mathematics meets the machine

My work blends rigorous dynamical-systems theory with modern machine learning to model, analyze, and predict the behavior of complex nonlinear systems.

∂

Scientific Machine Learning

Physics-informed neural networks and data-driven methods that embed governing equations directly into learning.

∞

Mathematical Biology

Predator–prey, epidemic, and population models — analyzed through high-codimension bifurcation theory.

∇

Nonlinear Dynamics

Reaction–diffusion systems, pattern formation, and the global dynamics of nonlinear differential equations.
